Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026 | Last updated: April 24, 2026

Shot Shaper ("the App") is developed and operated by Super Dev Studio, LLC ("Developer," "we," "us," or "our"). Your privacy is important, and this policy explains what data the App handles, how it is stored, your rights, and how to contact us.

Data Controller

For the purposes of applicable data protection laws (including the EU General Data Protection Regulation and the UK General Data Protection Regulation), the data controller is:

Super Dev Studio, LLC
Email: shotshaper@superdevstudio.com

Data We Do NOT Collect

Shot Shaper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with the Developer or any third parties. Specifically:

  • We do not operate any servers or backend services.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, health data, or any other sensitive device permissions.
  • We do not use device identifiers (IDFA or otherwise) for tracking or advertising.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Your swing fault and fix preferences
  • Custom faults and fixes you create
  • Practice session history (dates, faults visited, fixes used)
  • App settings and preferences (left-handed mode, club type, streak data, etc.)
  • Pro purchase status (a simple yes/no flag stored in UserDefaults on your device — no payment or billing data)

This data never leaves your device unless you enable iCloud sync (see below).

Widget Data

The App may share a small subset of your data (such as your current fix cues) with the Shot Shaper home-screen and lock-screen widgets via an App Group shared container. This data transfer occurs entirely on your device and is never sent to any external server.

iCloud Sync

If you are a Pro user, the App can sync your data across your Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your private iCloud account, encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple, and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. The Developer has no access to your iCloud data. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device's Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud.

Apple Watch

The App sends your fix cue data to the companion Apple Watch app using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This data is transferred directly between your paired iPhone and Apple Watch over a secure Bluetooth/Wi-Fi connection and is not sent to any external server.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time, non-recurring Pro upgrade for $3.99 (USD) through Apple's App Store using StoreKit 2. All purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. The Developer does not receive or store any payment information, billing details, or financial data. There are no subscriptions and no automatic renewals.

Notifications

The App may request your permission to send local notifications (practice reminders and streak nudges). These are local notifications — they are scheduled and generated entirely on your device without any server involvement. No notification tokens are collected, and no notification data is sent to any external server.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Shot Shaper does not include any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, crash reporting services, or tracking technologies. The App does not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) authorization because no tracking of any kind occurs. The App does not participate in any ad networks or data broker arrangements.

App Store Privacy Nutrition Label

In accordance with Apple's App Store requirements, we declare that Shot Shaper does not collect any data linked to your identity or used to track you. If you see a "Data Not Collected" label on our App Store listing, that accurately reflects the App's behavior.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Because all data processing occurs locally on your device and no personal data is transmitted to us, we do not process personal data in the traditional sense. To the extent any on-device data processing is considered subject to the GDPR or UK GDPR, the legal bases are:

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): The App processes your inputs locally to deliver the service you requested when you downloaded and began using the App (swing fault diagnosis and fix recommendations).
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): Where you opt in to local notifications or enable iCloud sync.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): For ancillary on-device processing such as remembering your preferences and session history to improve your experience.

Your Rights (GDPR / EEA and UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights regarding your data under the GDPR and UK GDPR respectively:

  • Access and Portability: All your data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account. You have full access to it at all times.
  • Rectification: You can edit or update any of your data directly within the App.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can delete all App data by deleting the App from your device. To remove iCloud data, go to Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage and delete Shot Shaper data.
  • Restriction and Objection: Because no data is transmitted to us, you can restrict processing by simply not using the App or by deleting it.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on your consent (e.g., notifications, iCloud sync), you can withdraw consent at any time by disabling those features in your device settings. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing performed before withdrawal.
  • Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

Your Rights (CCPA/CPRA / California Users)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (collectively, "CCPA"), grants you certain rights:

  • Categories of personal information collected: None. The App does not collect personal information as defined by the CCPA.
  • Sale or sharing of personal information: We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA. We have never sold or shared personal information and have no plans to do so.
  • Right to know and delete: Because no personal data is transmitted to us, there is nothing for us to disclose or delete. All data resides on your device under your control.
  • Right to correct: You can edit any of your data directly in the App at any time.
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information: We do not collect sensitive personal information.
  • Non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Shot Shaper is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. The App does not collect personal information from any user, including children under 13. Because no data is collected or transmitted, the App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow provided personal information through the App, please contact us at shotshaper@superdevstudio.com and we will assist you.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device (and optionally in your private iCloud account). We do not retain any copy of your data on any server.

  • To delete all App data: Delete the Shot Shaper app from your device. This permanently removes all locally stored data.
  • To delete iCloud data: Go to your device Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage > Shot Shaper, and tap "Delete Data."
  • To delete widget data: Removing the widget or deleting the App clears all shared widget data.
  • To delete Apple Watch data: Delete the Shot Shaper Watch app from your Apple Watch, or unpair your Apple Watch, to remove all Watch-side data.

International Data Transfers

The App does not transfer any data internationally. If you enable iCloud sync, Apple may store your iCloud data in data centers in various countries in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. The Developer has no control over or access to this process.
