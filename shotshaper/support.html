Getting Started

Open the app, pick your club type, and swipe through the fault cards. When you see your miss, tap "That's My Miss" to see fix cues. Swipe through the cues and tap "This Worked" when one clicks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch between left-handed and right-handed cues?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and toggle "Left-Handed Mode." All cues will automatically mirror for your stance.

What does Pro include?

  • Unlimited custom faults and fixes
  • Full session history with stats and coaching insights
  • Pre-round briefing
  • iCloud sync across devices
  • Apple Watch full fault deck

Pro is a one-time $3.99 purchase (USD). There is no subscription, no recurring charges, and no expiration. You pay once and keep Pro features forever. The purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store and is subject to Apple's standard Terms and Conditions.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." Your Pro status is tied to your Apple ID and will restore on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

If restore does not work:

  • Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID you originally used to purchase Pro.
  • Check your internet connection and try again.
  • Restart the app and attempt the restore again.
  • If the issue persists, contact Apple Support at support.apple.com or email us at shotshaper@superdevstudio.com.

Does the Apple Watch app work without Pro?

Yes. Free users see a quick glance with their top 3 fixes. Pro users get the full interactive fault deck on their wrist.

How does iCloud sync work?

Pro users' data syncs automatically via iCloud. Make sure iCloud is enabled in your device settings and that you are signed into the same Apple ID on all devices.

Can I create my own faults and fixes?

Yes. Go to Settings and use the Custom Fault Builder. Free users can create 1 custom fault. Pro users get unlimited.

How do I manage or disable practice reminders?

The app may ask permission to send local notifications for practice reminders and streak nudges. You can disable these at any time by going to your device Settings > Notifications > Shot Shaper and toggling notifications off. You can also manage reminder preferences within the app's Settings screen.

Is there a home-screen widget?

Yes. Shot Shaper offers home-screen and lock-screen widgets that display your current fix cues at a glance. To add one, long-press your home screen, tap the "+" button, and search for "Shot Shaper."

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. If you need a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's refund process. The developer cannot process refunds directly.

Deleting Your Data

Shot Shaper stores all data locally on your device. To delete your data:

  • Delete all App data: Delete the Shot Shaper app from your device. This permanently removes all locally stored data, including session history, custom faults, and preferences.
  • Delete iCloud data (Pro users): Go to your device Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage > Shot Shaper, and tap "Delete Data."
  • Delete Apple Watch data: Delete the Shot Shaper Watch app from your Apple Watch.
  • Delete widget data: Remove any Shot Shaper widgets from your home screen or lock screen. Widget data is also cleared when the app is deleted.

Because we do not operate any servers, there is no account to delete and no data stored remotely by the developer. Deleting the app removes everything.
